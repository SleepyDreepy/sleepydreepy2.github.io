Theme And features

Below is an explanation of the theme of the website along with it's features

Theme

  • The website was created with the theme "Living Contradiction" in mind
  • The background of the webpages is a character called "Monokuma" from the popular anime/game Danganronpa. This character is an enigma in and of itself as it is a cute teddy bear that wishes for nothing but the world's despair.
  • This is perfect for the website because people will subconsciously think that this portfolio would take on a darker tone. This would play into thinking that my personality test would bear much darker fruit.
  • The colors used, red, black and white, aid in giving off the feeling of a living contradiction.
  • The target audience for this website are people between the ages of 16-25
